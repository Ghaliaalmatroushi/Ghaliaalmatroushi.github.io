Welcome to NYU AlumNet!

Created by Ghalia Almatroushi, our website aims to connect high school students in the UAE with their high school alumni and provide insights into the NYU Abu Dhabi experience. Our mission is to bridge the gap between current students and alumni, allowing them to connect, learn from each other, and explore the possibilities offered by NYU Abu Dhabi.


At NYU AlumNet, we understand that high school students often seek guidance and first-hand experiences when considering their university options. Through our platform, we provide a simple and structured format that contains the content students typically look for. From information about academic programs, campus facilities, and student life to personal stories and experiences shared by alumni, we strive to give prospective students a comprehensive overview of the NYU Abu Dhabi community.


Additionally, NYU AlumNet plays a vital role in university fairs held in high schools across the UAE. By offering a concise and accessible resource, we aim to facilitate introductions to NYU Abu Dhabi, enabling students to quickly grasp the essence of the university and its unique offerings.


Join us on this exciting journey of connecting, learning, and discovering the NYU Abu Dhabi experience through the eyes of our alumni. Explore the rich insights and vibrant community that awaits you!
