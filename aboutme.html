About Me

Ghalia Almatroushi

Currently, I am a third-year student at NYU Abu Dhabi, majoring in Interactive Media and pursuing minors in Computer Science. My passion for the sciences, particularly space science, runs deep, and I aspire to merge my technological expertise with the world of science in the future. Recently, I made the decision to switch my major to Interactive Media, a field that captivates me with its novelty and boundless possibilities for experimentation and learning. Moreover, I find the community surrounding this discipline to be incredibly supportive and inspiring.
Beyond academics, I find joy in engaging in various sports, with horse riding being a particular favorite of mine. Additionally, I am an avid reader and cherish moments spent with my family. Through my personal portfolio, I aim to offer you a glimpse into my personality, interests, and, hopefully, the trajectory I am forging for myself.
