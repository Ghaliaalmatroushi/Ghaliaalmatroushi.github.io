Chosen Alumni: Ghalia Almatroushi

Quote:

As a high school alumni who chose NYU Abu Dhabi, I can confidently say that the experience has been nothing short of amazing. Every day is filled with new adventures and discoveries. From the vibrant marketplace bustling with flavors and cultures, to the captivating art center showcasing creativity at its finest, and the tranquil library cafe where ideas come to life, the campus is a hub of constant inspiration. This short video clip is just a glimpse of the places I visit on a daily basis, and the places where I meet up and catch up with friends, faculty, and staff members. What truly sets NYU Abu Dhabi apart is the sense of community and belonging. Despite being far from home, I immediately felt embraced by the warmth and support of fellow students and faculty. The diverse backgrounds and perspectives enrich every interaction, creating an inclusive environment that feels like a second home. Choosing NYU Abu Dhabi was a decision that has forever shaped my journey. It has provided me with unforgettable experiences, lifelong friendships, and a global perspective that extends far beyond the classroom.
